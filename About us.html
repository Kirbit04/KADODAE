The Creator

Melody Wambui Njuguna, the creator of Kadodae Creations is a creative mind that put her creative powers to use in her time of Distress. During this time she was a young girl of age 16 who came up with a mesmerizing collection of Mordern African jewelry. She is a very dedicated and hardworking woman, always eager to add and improve her collection.
